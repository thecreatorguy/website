Who am I?

If I (Tim Johnson if you didn't catch that by now) were to choose three descriptions for myself, I would pick software engineer/math guy, someone who watches anime and cartoons more often than live-action television, and well-adjusted adult. I have a blog that you can read to find out more!

Currently my projects are few, but the gears are turning. You can find them on my projects page. In my personal free time, I like to play D&D (if my friends could ever find the time), play video games like Factorio, and maintain a blissful ignorance of pop culture and most social media platforms.

What do I want?

Everyone has an idea, and I would love to contribute not only to the development of that idea but to the design, planning, and expansion of that idea!

As for my personal goals, I'm currently in pursuit of self improvement. Among these are to branch out socially, become the best physical version of myself, and explore creative activities like writing, getting back into learning the piano, and expanding on/creating new personal projects.
